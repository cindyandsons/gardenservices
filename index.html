Our Story

We started our garden service in 2022, but our passion for beautiful outdoor spaces began long before that. With years of prior experience in much bigger landscaping and tree felling jobs for Cindy's ex-husband, we built a solid foundation of expertise.

When work paused unexpectedly, Cindy and her eldest son started taking on smaller jobs just to keep afloat.
