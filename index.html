Our Story

Started in 2022, Cindy & Sons Garden Services emerged from years of experience in larger landscaping and tree felling projects. After a pause in operations, Cindy and her eldest son Kyle decided to keep the business afloat by taking on smaller jobs.

What began as a way to stay active in the industry has blossomed into a thriving service that Cape Town residents love. Our clients appreciate our no-fuss approach, reliability, and attention to detail.

We're a small family business with big ambitions to help maintain and improve gardens across Cape Town. Whether it's a quick tidy-up or a major landscaping project, we're here to help.
